Chicken Stir-Fry

Home

Description

This Chicken Stir-Fry is a quick and flavorful dish that brings together the essence of Asian cuisine with minimal effort. Start by heating sesame oil in a wok or large pan, where you'll first sauté minced garlic and grated ginger for a burst of aromatic flavors. Next, introduce thinly sliced chicken breast, cooking until it's nearly done but still tender. Then, add a colorful medley of vegetables like broccoli, bell peppers, and snap peas, stir-frying them until they achieve a delightful crisp-tender texture. A splash of soy sauce unifies the ingredients, providing that iconic umami taste. Serve this vibrant dish hot, perhaps over a bed of steamed rice, for a complete, satisfying meal.

ingredients

  • Chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • Mixed vegetables (broccoli, bell peppers, snap peas)
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic, minced
  • Ginger,grated
  • Sesame oil

Steps

  1. Heat sesame oil in a wok or large pan.
  2. Sauté garlic and ginger until fragrant.
  3. Add chicken slices, cook until nearly done.
  4. Toss in vegetables, stir-fry until they're crisp.
  5. Drizzle with soy sauce, cook for another minute.
